Rhiannon Navin

Professor At Cambridge University

Rhiannon Navin grew up in Bremen, Germany, in a family of book-crazy women. Her career in advertising brought her to New York City,
where she worked for several large agencies before becoming a full-time mother and writer.She now lives outside of New York City with
her husband, three children, two cats, and one dog. Only Child is her first novel.
